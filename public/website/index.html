About Tailmates

Tailmates is more than just a pet app - it's a community of animal lovers who believe that pets deserve the best social life too. We connect pet owners, facilitate playdates, and create lasting friendships between pets and their owners.

Whether you have a playful puppy, a curious cat, or any other furry friend, Tailmates helps you find the perfect companions for your pet's social needs.
